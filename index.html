Why Say No to Drugs?

Understanding the risks is the first step to making better choices

Damages Your Mind

Drugs alter brain chemistry, leading to memory loss, poor judgment, and mental health disorders that can last a lifetime.

Harms Your Body

From heart disease to liver failure, drugs destroy your physical health and can lead to permanent damage or death.

Risks Your Future

Addiction can lead to financial ruin, broken relationships, legal troubles, and lost opportunities.

The Real Consequences

Drug abuse doesn't just affect you - it impacts everyone around you

  • Permanent damage to brain development (especially in teens)
  • Increased risk of overdose and accidental death
  • Loss of trust from family and friends
  • Legal consequences including jail time
  • Difficulty holding a job or staying in school
  • Financial problems from supporting the addiction
